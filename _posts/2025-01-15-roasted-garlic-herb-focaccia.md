This focaccia is a staple in our house. Edward has strong opinions about anything that comes out of the oven; this one passes the test.

## Ingredients

- 4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1¾ cups warm water
- 2 tsp active dry yeast
- 2 tsp kosher salt
- 1 head roasted garlic, cloves squeezed out
- Fresh rosemary and thyme, chopped
- Olive oil and flaky salt for topping

## Instructions

1. Combine yeast and warm water; let sit until foamy. Mix in flour, salt, and half the roasted garlic. Knead until smooth; rise until doubled.
2. Press into an oiled half-sheet pan. Dimple with your fingers, drizzle with olive oil, and top with remaining garlic and herbs. Sprinkle with flaky salt.
3. Bake at 425°F until golden, about 25 minutes. Let cool slightly before slicing.
